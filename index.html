Car Collector!

How to play?

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four car logos below. By clicking on a logo you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matchiing your total score to random number, you lose th game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each logo is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each logo.
